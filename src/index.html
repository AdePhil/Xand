FIFTH EDITION

Porridge Yam

(Asaro)

Serving: 4

1/2 tuber yam (medium)

1 cup shredded smoked chicken

1/2 cup dry oporo

2 knoor cube

1 tbsp crayfish grounded

8 hobonero peper

1 onion

2 cksp palm oil

Prep Time: < 7min

peel yam, cube it, wash and set aside

Wash and shredd pepper roughly and aside

Wash oporo and soak then set aside
